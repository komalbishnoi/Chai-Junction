"Make your second home and never feel alone."

Chai Junction (CJ) is a startup tea café in Ottawa opened by two graduates from Algonquin college, the inspiration behind the brand was unavailability of tea shops than the coffee shops in the area and the increasing rate of Indian students at the campus. CJ aims to provide quality tea and the feeling of being at homeland and a place to sit together and discover new people.
